There are 216 Colors!

This can be determined by dividing the range of each color in 20% increments. This creates muliples of 51 decimal or 33 hexidecimal. In hexidecimal this represents 00=0%, 33=20%, 66=40%, 99=60%, CC=80%, FF=100%.

Visualize dicing the Color Cube into six sections per dimension. this equates to 6 x 6 x 6 = 216
