IndexCatman's XHTML 1.1 Elements and Attributes Reference Guide

This document provides a list of legal XHTML 1.1 elements arranged according to category: Block, Form, Head, Heading, HTML, Inline, List, and Table. At the end of the document, you'll find a descriptive list of the allowed attributes.
