Oasis of Peace - Look into heart

  • Can you say? No Rancor, no bitterness, no turmoil, no conflict, no nervousness, no tension
  • Symptoms not at peace: tense, can't remember, hurried, can't concentrate.
  • Prayer spreads the oasis of peace

Awareness exercise

  1. Close eyes
  2. Get feel of touch of clothes on shoulders
  3. Get feel of your: back, hands, thighs, feet
  4. Once again for a total of three times

  5. Feel tense: what part of body is tense, become aware and melt the tension.

  6. Gently open your eyes

Story: God tired of people pestering him. He wants to hide.
Angels tell him hide on the highest mountain, depths of ocean, far side of moon.
Hide in human heart, they will never think of searching for you there.

Awareness exercise brings you to your heart, brings you home.
Find peace but need time.

Alternative Awareness exercise for busy people

  1. Driving get the feel of your body in the car. Feel your back, hands, smell
  2. Walking get the feel of your body walking. Transferring weight.

Perspective Exercise

  1. Make list of things for which I am possessive.
  2. Say to your self, "This too will pass away".
  3. Make list of things for which I dislike or fear.
  4. Say to your self, "This too will pass away".

Story of a Guru: Just as a fish will perish on dry land. So will you perish if you get tangled in worldly affairs.
The fish must get back to the water if it wishes to live.
So must you get back to your heart. (Go home to the present)

Slowdown Exercises

  1. Do one thing at a time.
  2. Give yourself more time to do daily routines.
  3. Verbalize every action you make.
  4. Will bring you to present. Makes you centered and integrated.

Japanese Proverb: The day you stop traveling (rushing) you will arrive.

Museum Parody Story: Hurry up if you stop to look at everything you will see nothing.

Driving Story: Wife looks at map. Darling we are on the wrong road. Never mind we are making great time.

Jesus says: You gained the world, but lost your soul.

God and spirituality are found in life, not withdrawal ( Message of Christianity).

Acceptance Exercise

Lord give me the grace to change whatever can be changed,
to accept what can not be changed,
and the wisdom to know the difference.

  1. 95% of things that upset us cannot be changed.
  2. Stand before each of these things you cannot change and say yes to God's will.
May the peace of God which surpasses all understanding, guard our hearts and thoughts in Christ Jesus our lord.
