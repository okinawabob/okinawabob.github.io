Summary

I was introduced to Anthony deMello back in 1990 when I attended a contemplative prayer group at Sacred Heart Catholic Church in Calumet, Michigan.

I was going through one of those phases in life when you wonder what is the meaning of life. I had achieved most of the goals one typically sets in life with education and career. I was comparitively rich as an engineer, since I could make as much money as I wanted to buy things that I wanted. But life seemed a bit shallow living the "American Dream".

I thought perhaps religion might have the answers so I read many books from Christianity, Buddhist, Hindu, and Cabalhistic (Jewish Mysticism) faiths. I noticed many similarities but resisted being swallowed by the dogma of any one faith.

For me, Anthony deMello presented the best insight into happiness and feeling God's presense in one's life. He grew up in India and is a Catholic Jesuit Priest. He teaches in a story telling manner and yet finds the common thread.

This web page contains audio from his first video "A Way to God for Today". You can purchase his videos and books by following the links below. I hope you will find as much inspiration in his words as I have.
