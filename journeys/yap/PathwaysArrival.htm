Getting to Yap and Arrival
Yap Lies in western part of the Federated States of Micronesia. Air transportation is currently provided by Continental Airlines. Flights to Yap are a stopover on the Guam-Palau flights. The flights are relatively short, only one hour from Guam and 45 minutes from Palau.
Airport transfers are free. Simply let us know your scheduled flight number and date of arrival and we will pick you up at the airport. You can also call us upon arrival.
