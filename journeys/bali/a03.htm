Lake Braten is a beautiful lake in the interior. And of course to celebrate it the Balinese have built a beautiful temple on the shores of this beautiful mountain lake. No I'm not wearing a skirt it is a sarong. Kind of like an Balinese version of a Kilt except everyone man or women wears them. Much cooler then trousers. If you go to a temple you need to wear a sarong to fit in.
